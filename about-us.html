About Us

Introduction

Total Recycling Limited is a locally established company in Trinidad & Tobago. The company would like to give a suitable monetary value to all customers (small as well as industrial) for their recyclable waste which otherwise might have gone to landfill. This would help in inculcating the "Culture of Recycling" among the people of Trinidad & Tobago. The company intends to change waste materials into new products to prevent waste of potentially useful materials, reduce the consumption of fresh raw materials, reduce energy usage, reduce air and water pollution and lower green house gas emissions.

Our Story

I, the promoter of Total Recycling Limited after working thirty-six years in corporate world, wanted to do something for the society and for future generation by giving them a better environment. This social cause would also serve as an economically viable activity.To achieve this I have decided to form a recycling company in Trinidad & Tobago. To be honest, Trinidad & Tobago is a country which always excites me for its culture, warmth of local people and also for the high level of industrialization.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to create an environment where our partners, associates, buyers, sellers, service providers and fellow employees take pride in dealing with the company.

Objectives

Total Recycle Limited is formed with an objective to take the recycling activity in Trinidad & Tobago to an another level by adopting the different ways of recycling and processes.
